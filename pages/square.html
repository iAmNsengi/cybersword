About the Square CTF

About Square

Square is a financial services platform developed by Block, Inc. It is aimed at small-and medium-sized businesses, allowing them to accept credit card payments and use phones or tablets as payment registers for a point-of-sale system. (Wikipedia)
—
Square processed $45.4 billion in gross payment volume (GPV) in Q3 2022. (insiderintelligence.com)

Flag format

You know you have successfully solved a puzzle if you find a string which matches the following regular expression: /flag\{.*\}/i.

For instance, flag{27fbe50bf1ccdaf5cfc9a1} would be a valid flag.
